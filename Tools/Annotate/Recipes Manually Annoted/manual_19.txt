For the bhaji 

<CA>Boil</CA> the <I>cauliflower</I>, <I>peas</I> and <I>carrots</I> till they are soft. <CA>Drain out</CA> the excess <I>water.</I>

<CA>Add</CA> the <I>tomatoes</I> and <CA>simmer</CA> till the <iI>oil</iI> separates.

<CA>Heat</CA> a large <U>tava</U> and <CA>cook</CA> the pav on both sides till the pieces are lightly browned.
