Sieve the <I>flour.</I> 
<I>Rub</I> 1 tablespoon of <I>margarine</I> very well into the <I>flour.</I> 
Make a well in the centre. Add the <I>salt</I> in this well and pour a little ice-cold <I>water</I> on top. 
Make a soft <I>dough</I> by adding some more ice-cold <I>water.</I> 
Knead the <I>dough</I> for 2 minutes. 
Keep the <I>dough</I> under a cloth for 10 minutes. 
<I>Roll</I> out the <I>dough</I> with the help of a little <I>flour</I> to a rectangular shape. Brush away the <I>extra</I> <I>flour.</I> 
Apply the remaining 115 grams of <I>margarine</I> leaving aside a strip of 12 mm. (1/2") width all around. 
Divide the <I>dough</I> into 4 imaginary equal parts as in picture. 
Fold part no.1 on the left on part no.2. 
Next fold part no.4 on the right on part no.3. 
Next fold part no.3 on part no.2. 
Sprinkle plenty of <I>flour</I> on a tray. Put the <I>rolled</I> <I>dough</I> over it and sprinkle <I>flour</I> again. cover with a cloth. 
Chill the <I>dough</I> in the freezer compartment of a refrigerator over the <I>ice</I> tray for 30 minutes. 
Remove from the refrigerator and <I>roll</I> out to a rectangular shape. 
Divide the <I>dough</I> into 3 imaginary equal parts as under. 
Fold part no.1 on the left on part no.2 and fold part no.3 on the right on part no.2. 
Chill the <I>dough</I> in the freezer compartment of a refrigerator over the <I>ice</I> tray for 15 minutes. 
Repeat steps 15, 16 and 17. 
<I>Roll</I> out the <I>dough</I> to about 6 mm. (1/4") thickness in the shape of rectangle. 
Cut into squares. 
Put a little <I>stuffing</I> in the centre of each squares and make a shape of triangle out of it by pressing together two opposite diagonal corners. 
Arrange on a baking dish. Brush with <I>ghee.</I> 
Bake in a hot oven at 450°F for 15 minutes. 
Bake in hot oven at 450°F for 15 minutes. Reduce the temperature to 350°F and bake for a further 10 minutes. 
Serve hot.
