Sprinkle <I>yeast</I> and <I>sugar</I> over <I>warm water.</I> 

Keep aside for 5 minutes or till frothy. 

Mix <I>salt</I> and <I>flour,</I> take in a large plate. 

Make well in centre. Put <I>curds</I> in well. 

Sprinkle <I>baking powder</I> over <I>curds.</I> 

Allow to stand for 2-3 minutes. 

Pour <I>oil,</I> <I>yeast</I> solution, in well and mix gradually. 

Use <I>milk</I> as required, to make a soft stiff <I>dough.</I> 

Brush <I>dough</I> will <I>oil.</I> Cover with inverted bowl. 

Keep aside for 4 hours. Longer if weather is cold. 

Grease palms well and punch <I>dough</I> till soft and elastic. 

Cover again and keep aside for 10 minutes. 

Break of a lump of <I>dough.</I> 

<I>Roll</I> or pat to a thick <I>round</I> about 6" diam. 

<I>Roast</I> on hot griddle, or tandoor, till golden spots appear. 

Or shallow fry on griddle. 

Serve hot with gravied <I>vegetables,</I> dals, etc.
