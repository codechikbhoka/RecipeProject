Liquidise 1 of the <I>onions</I> with the <I>garlic,</I> <I>pepper,</I> <I>salt,</I> <I>coriander</I> and <I>turmeric.</I> 
In a pan melt the <I>ghee</I> and fry the remaining <I>onion</I> till golden. Add the liquidised <I>onion</I> mix and cook for 5 minutes. 
Cut the <I>okra</I> in half crossways, gently add this to the pot so as not to break them up too much. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 20 minutes. 
Add the <I>tomatoes,</I> <I>mint</I> and <I>garam masala</I> and cook for another 15 minutes.
