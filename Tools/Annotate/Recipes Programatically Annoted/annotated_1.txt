Heat <I>oil</I> in a 10-inch heavy skillet over moderate heat until hot but not smoking. Add <I>carrots</I> and saut, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes. Add <I>pomegranate juice,</I> <I>cinnamon stick,</I> <I>salt,</I> and <I>pepper</I> and simmer, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until <I>carrots</I> are tender and <I>liquid</I> is reduced to a <I>glaze,</I> 20 to 25 minutes. Add <I>coriander seeds</I> and cook, stirring, 1 minute. Discard <I>cinnamon stick</I> and serve <I>carrots</I> hot or at room temperature.
